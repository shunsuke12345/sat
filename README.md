Overview

SAT Vocab Battle Royale is an educational web-based game designed to help students improve their SAT vocabulary through a competitive battle royale experience. Instead of memorizing word lists, students answer vocabulary questions while competing against 99 AI opponents. The goal is to survive, build streaks, climb the leaderboard, and become the last vocabulary champion standing.

Problem

Many students preparing for the Digital SAT struggle with vocabulary because:

SAT vocabulary contains thousands of difficult words.
Traditional flashcards and memorization can be repetitive and boring.
Students often lack motivation to practice consistently.
Learned words are easily forgotten without regular reinforcement.
Weak vocabulary can negatively impact SAT Reading and Writing scores.
Solution

SAT Vocab Battle Royale transforms vocabulary practice into an engaging game experience.

Students can:

Learn SAT vocabulary anytime and anywhere.
Practice through interactive multiple-choice questions.
Compete against AI opponents in real-time simulations.
Receive immediate feedback on answers.
Improve retention through repetition and active recall.
Stay motivated with rankings, streaks, and survival mechanics.
Features
Battle Royale Gameplay
100 players enter each match.
Only one vocabulary champion survives.
Players are eliminated as the game progresses.
SAT Vocabulary Database
Hundreds of SAT-level vocabulary words.
Definitions, synonyms, antonyms, and contextual usage.
Multiple question types for varied learning.
Health System
Players begin with 100 HP.
Incorrect answers reduce HP.
Running out of HP results in elimination.
Live Leaderboard
Track rankings throughout the match.
Compare performance against other players.
Compete for the top position.
Streak System
Consecutive correct answers increase streaks.
Higher streaks reward better scores.
Encourages consistent accuracy.
Time-Based Scoring
Each question has a countdown timer.
Faster responses earn bonus points.
Adds excitement and challenge.

How It Works
Step 1: Enter the Arena

Step 2: Answer Questions

Synonyms

Fill-in-the-blank questions
Step 3: Survive

Correct answers:

Increase score
Build streaks
Improve survival chances

Incorrect answers:

Reduce HP
Increase risk of elimination
Step 4: Climb the Leaderboard

Players compete against AI opponents and attempt to become the final survivor.
